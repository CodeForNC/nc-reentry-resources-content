Miscellaneous Resources

  • The Household Budgeting Worksheet from the budgeting section of ConsumerCredit.com can help with responsible financial planning.
  • The State Health & Human Services page links to all county HHS websites. The sites offers information on Public Assistance, Public Health, Social Work Services, Veterans Benefits, children services, Medicaid and more.
  • The FTC Consumer Website provides information ranging from jobs and money making tips, identity theft prevention, home buying and renting, health and fitness, and money and credit strategies.
  • The FTC Facts for Consumers publication on the Fair Credit Billing Act offers information on how to dispute credit card charges and billing errors.
  • Assurance Wireless offers free phones and limited free voice/text to certain low-income households who qualify.
