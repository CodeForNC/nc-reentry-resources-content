Connecting with a healthcare professional as soon as possible after release from incarceration is an important step. Restoration of Medicaid and Medicare benefits does not occur automatically.

If you are not eligible for Medicaid or Medicare, you can obtain health insurance under the Affordable Care Act (ACA).

Release from detention constitutes a qualifying life event under the ACA and entitles the person to apply for health coverage on the Marketplace. Applicants must apply within 60 days of release. Medicaid, Medicare, and the ACA can be applied for all at the same time.

More information is available at https://www.healthcare.gov or call 1-855-733-3711 to schedule an appointment with a Federal Navigator.

The Federal Interagency ReEntry Council offers a helpful resource that dispels common myths about what federal benefits those recently released from incarceration may be able to access.
